Bio

I am currently a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at the Illinois Institute of Technology (IIT) guided by Prof. Yan Yan. My research is centered on Efficient AI, with a specific focus on Dataset Distillation. Before Ph.D., I received my M.S. in Computer Science from the Illinois Institute of Technology (IIT) and a B.S. in Computer Science and Engineering from Nanjing University of Post and Telecommunications (NJUPT).
